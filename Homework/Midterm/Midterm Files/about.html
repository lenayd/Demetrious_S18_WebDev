THE STORY

Zoo Crew Two is a feature-length screenplay written by father-daughter duo Michael and Lenay Demetrious. Based on a true story, the film centers around 16-year-old Michael, a child actor who decides to quit his career to become a gang leader. Michael assembles his gang with 12 of his ragtag neighborhood accomplices and, naturally, trouble begins to brew among the streets of 1979 Astoria, Queens. Stay tuned to find out how this trouble manifests and how the story progresses.
